CanvasGamer is small game development studio. Our goal is to provide entertainment for the people, and help the future game developers by providing our source code from our games.

As a big fans of gaming, we played countless games in our lives, we found them very impressive and intriguing. When we were little we always wanted to make games of our own, but back then we didn't have the technology and the knowledge to do so. That changed when we grew up and went into IT collage, now we are game developers and we enjoy doing it. We are making this small games as a code example, to the new gaming developers who want to follow our footsteps and want to learn, we think if we help people they will help others which will contribute in general to better game production and more availability.
